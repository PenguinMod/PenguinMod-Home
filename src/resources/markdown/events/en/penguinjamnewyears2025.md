# Welcome to New Year's PenguinJam 2025!

The theme has not been revealed yet! Come back here when the Game Jam starts.

## Jam Duration:
December 21, 5:00 PM EST - January 1, 5:00 PM EST

## Submissions:
Upload your game and make sure to include the hashtag [#nypjam25](/search?q=%23nypjam25) in your project description!

## Rewards:
First Place: Gold PenguinJam Badge
Second Place: Silver PenguinJam Badge
Third Place: Bronze PenguinJam Badge

First, Second, and Third also get the Event Winners Badge.

Good luck! Make sure to upload your project with the hashtag before the deadline!
